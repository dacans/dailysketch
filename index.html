This Privacy Policy describes how DailySketch ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Sketch Photos: When you upload photos of your drawings, they are stored locally on your device only.
  • Completion Data: Information about which prompts you've completed and when.
  • Preferences: Your app settings, such as notification preferences, favorite prompts, and theme selection.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility.
  • Purchase Information: Through RevenueCat and Apple, we process in-app purchases. We receive anonymous purchase receipts to verify premium status.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Core Features: Display your completed prompts, track your streak, and store your sketch gallery.
  • Process Purchases: Verify premium subscription status and manage in-app purchases through RevenueCat and Apple's payment system.
  • Send Notifications: Deliver daily reminders if you enable them (you can opt out anytime).
  • Improve the App: Understand which features are most used to prioritize improvements and fix bugs.
  • Maintain Security: Detect and prevent technical issues, abuse, or fraud.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage First: Your sketch photos and completion data are stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage system. We don't upload your creative work to any server.

Security Measures:

  • Encryption: All data stored on your device is protected by iOS's built-in encryption.
  • Secure Connections: Any communication with our servers (for premium verification) uses HTTPS encryption.
  • No Cloud Backup: Your photos are not automatically backed up to our servers (unless you use iCloud, which is controlled by your iOS settings).
  • Limited Access: Only essential third-party services (RevenueCat for purchases) have access to anonymized data.

4. Third-Party Services

DailySketch integrates with the following third-party services:

4.1 RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)

  • Purpose: Process and manage in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Data Shared: Anonymous user ID, purchase receipts, subscription status
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

4.2 Apple App Store

  • Purpose: Handle payment processing and purchase receipts
  • Data Shared: Payment information is handled entirely by Apple (we never see your credit card details)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4.3 Apple Push Notification Service (APNs)

  • Purpose: Deliver daily reminder notifications (only if you opt in)
  • Data Shared: Device token for notifications (no personal information)

6. Children's Privacy

DailySketch is suitable for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. All data is stored locally on the device, and we do not require account creation or personal details.

Parents should review this Privacy Policy with their children and supervise app usage as they see fit.
